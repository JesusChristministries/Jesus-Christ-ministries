Today's Devotional

November 14, 2023

"For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son, that whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life."

John 3:16 (NIV)

The message of John 3:16 resonates in every language and culture around the world. It is perhaps the most well-known verse in Scripture, yet its profundity can never be exhausted. The radical love of God displayed in the giving of His Son transcends cultural boundaries and speaks to the deepest need of every human heart.

As we carry this message to the nations, we are not bringing a foreign concept but the very truth for which every soul was created. God's love is not limited by geography, language, or cultural background. It is the universal remedy for the universal human condition.

Today, consider how this fundamental truth—that God gave His Son for you—shapes your daily life. How does it motivate your interactions with others, especially those from different backgrounds? How might God be calling you to participate in His global mission of love?

Dear Father, thank You for Your boundless love that crossed the greatest divide to reach us. Help us to comprehend the width, length, height, and depth of Your love in Christ, and empower us to share this love with people from every nation, tribe, and tongue. Make us ambassadors of Your reconciliation in this divided world. In Jesus' name, Amen.
